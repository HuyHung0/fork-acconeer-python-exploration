A121 Detectors
==============

A Detector process the radar data and produces a result, such as a distance or a presence detection.
Using a Detector gives you a head start since it offers ready-to-use results.

C implementations of Detectors are included in the `SDK <https://developer.acconeer.com/>`_ and is part of our product offering.
